Just like playing an instrument, knowing how to manage your money and credit better is a learned skill. But all too often it’s a skill that many of us haven’t had a chance to learn, let alone strengthen. Budget Buddy will help your youth learn about budgeting, how to do it, and why through an easy-to-understand simulation game. It will help you fine tune your money skills and increase your knowledge about personal finances.

Record your income, expenditures, and how much you want to save, and we'll help you understand your next steps.

Before starting, it's helpful to think about: your attitude towards money, how you feel about the state of your finances, and what you hope to achieve through this simulation.
