What's a Budget?

The official definition of a budget is "an estimate of income and expenditure for a set period of time".

This means it is a process of creating a plan to spend your money. Creating this plan will allow you determine in advance whether you'll have enough moeny to do the things you want to do.

Why build a budget?

Your money can sometimes seem like a puzzle. Money wil be coming in and out of your bank account regularly, but until you write it down, it will be difficult to see what's truly going on. This can lead to worry when making financial decisions. They key to getting and staying in control is by creating a balanced budget.

How do you build a Budget?

This website will give you a basic understanding on budgeting, what types of expenditures we spend on, why and how to save, and get you started on creating a budget. This will be done through our simulation game which wil lget you started by helping you understand how you spend your moeny, and what to do from there.
